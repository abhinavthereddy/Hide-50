Steganography

Steganography is the art and science of writing hidden messages in such a way that no one, apart from the sender and intended recipient, suspects the existence of the message, a form of security through obscurity. Consequently, functions provided by Stéganô only hide messages, without encryption. Steganography is often used with cryptography. Hide50 implements a non-complex encyption based on Caesar from problem set 2 of this course prior to actual steganography taking place. User is required to provide a key (Integer between 1 and 26, both included).
